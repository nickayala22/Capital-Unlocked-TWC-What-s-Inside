Capital Unlocked: The Training Program

5 weeks, hands-on, live. Two coaching calls per week. You build your entire capital-raising infrastructure in real time, working your actual raise, not watching someone explain theory.

+
Week 1 · Foundation
The W.A.R. System
Warm outreach, Anchored positioning, Relationship velocity. Your outreach engine launches Week 1. You leave with a running pipeline, not a framework you'll think about later.
+
Week 2 · Positioning
Investor-Ready Pitch Deck
We build and pressure-test the actual deck you'll use. Not a template you fill in alone. A real presentation reviewed against what institutional investors look for before they commit capital.
+
Week 2 · Clarity
Offer Clarity Framework
Most operators can't answer "what are you raising, why now, why you" in 90 seconds. This forces that clarity. You'll know exactly what to say and mean it.
+
Week 3 · Outreach
Investor Outreach Engine
Databases, sequencing, warm intro strategy, cold frameworks that convert. By Week 3 you have an active system running, not a list of names you're unsure how to approach.
+
Week 5 · Infrastructure
Data Room Build-Out
Track record docs, deal summaries, financials formatted for capital partners. The exact structure that makes an investor comfortable enough to wire. Most operators never build this. That's why they stall at soft commits.
+
Ongoing · Live
Live Weekly Coaching Calls
Two calls per week. Tuesday Capital Architecture sessions. Thursday onboarding and training. Real deals, real objections, real feedback in real time, not recorded content from six months ago.
+
Week 4 · Communication
Investor Call Mastery
Scripts, objection handling, live call transcripts reviewed in session. The language that keeps a conversation moving toward commitment. What not to say is often more valuable than any script.
+
Week 4 · Structure
Deal Structure Education
SAFEs, equity splits, promissory notes, preferred returns, hybrid structures. Enough mechanics to structure deals intelligently and have a real conversation with counsel before anyone signs.
+
Week 1 · Credibility
Track Record Positioning
Even operators with limited history have a story worth telling. We build a credible, defensible track record presentation that communicates experience and judgment without overstating what isn't there.
+
Week 5 · Closing
Closing the Wire
From soft commit to funded. The last 20% of a raise is where most deals fall apart. You'll know how to move from "I'm in" to wired funds, including follow-up sequencing, commitment tracking, and every step in between.

The Wealth Circle: The Private Network

A curated membership connecting operators with accredited investors, private lenders, and family offices. Access to introductions is earned through positioning, not just enrollment. The gate is intentional, it protects everyone inside.

🤝
Private Investor Network
+
Access to a curated network of accredited investors, private lenders, and family offices actively deploying capital. Not a directory. A vetted, living network where introductions happen when your deal and positioning meet the bar. Quality over volume, by design.
📁
LP Resources Vault
+
Due diligence checklists, investment playbooks, market guides, and LP-facing educational content. Know how capital partners evaluate deals and you'll structure yours to match what they're actually looking for.
📊
Curated Deal Flow
+
Members view active deal opportunities. Full access, including deal names, data rooms, and contact details, is tiered by membership level. Operators submit deals for review. The network sees what makes it through. Curated deal flow, not a bulletin board.
🎙️
Exclusive Member Sessions
+
Direct conversations with wealth advisors, institutional allocators, family office principals, and operators who've closed significant raises. Not recycled podcast content. Sessions built for people actively raising or deploying, not passively watching.
💬
VIP Investor Community
+
A private community for deal conversation, introductions, and peer accountability. Full members get access to the complete network chat, deal discussions, and direct lines to operators and capital partners moving in similar spaces.
🔍
Deal Readiness Review
+
Before your deal goes in front of the network, it gets reviewed. A bad intro damages everyone in the room. Members who go through this process show up positioned to close, not just positioned to pitch. This gate protects you and the network.
🌐
Gulf Region Capital Access
+
Through active relationships with US-UAE trade commissioners and Blue Harbor Group, TWC members have exposure to Gulf-region capital channels, including family offices and sovereign-adjacent capital deploying into US markets. Relationship-based access, not mass outreach.
📋
Past Deals Archive
+
A running archive of past deals that have moved through the network, visible to all members. No confidential details. Gives new members a real sense of deal velocity, structure, and the types of opportunities the network attracts.
