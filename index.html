Scripts, objection handling, live call transcripts reviewed in session, and the language that keeps a conversation moving toward commitment. What not to say is often more valuable than the script itself.

SAFEs, equity splits, promissory notes, preferred returns, hybrid structures. Mechanics you understand well enough to structure deals that make sense for both sides and have an informed conversation with counsel before signing.
▶

Even operators with limited history have a story to tell. We help you build a credible, defensible track record presentation that communicates experience, judgment, and execution, without overstating what isn't there.

From soft commit to funded. The last 20% of a raise is where most deals fall apart. You'll know how to move from "I'm in" to wired funds, including follow-up sequencing, commitment tracking, and the operational steps most people miss.
